Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Ahmedabad services

Do you only serve Ahmedabad?

Yes, currently PawfectMatches is focused on serving the Ahmedabad community. We connect local pet parents with adopters within the city to ensure easy meetups and smooth adoptions. We may expand to other cities in the future!

How do I adopt a pet in Ahmedabad?

Browse our available pets, select one you're interested in, and click "Request to Adopt". You'll be connected directly with the pet parent in Ahmedabad to discuss the adoption process and arrange a meetup.

Is delivery available for shop orders in Ahmedabad?

Yes! We deliver to all areas in Ahmedabad. Orders above ₹500 qualify for free delivery. Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days within the city.

Can I list my pet for adoption if I'm in Ahmedabad?

Absolutely! If you're in Ahmedabad and need to find a loving home for your pet, you can list them for free on PawfectMatches. Just create an account, fill in your pet's details, and we'll help you connect with potential adopters in the city.

Which areas in Ahmedabad do you cover?

We cover all areas in Ahmedabad including Satellite, Vastrapur, Bodakdev, Prahlad Nagar, SG Highway, Navrangpura, Paldi, Maninagar, Chandkheda, Gota, Bopal, Thaltej, Shilaj, Naranpura, Sabarmati, and many more!
